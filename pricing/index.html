Common Questions
Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are month-to-month. Cancel from your account dashboard and your listing deactivates at the end of the billing period.

I'm already in the directory but my listing is unclaimed — what do I do?

Find your listing in the directory and click "Claim this listing." You'll be walked through subscribing and setting up your full profile.

How do I update my profile after it's live?

Log in to your account and use the "Update Profile" form. Changes go live within one business day.

What's the difference between Listed and Featured?

Both give you a full profile. Featured adds priority placement at the top of category and city pages, plus homepage rotation and social media features.

Do you serve vendors outside Lafayette?

Yes — we cover all of Acadiana, including New Iberia, Opelousas, Breaux Bridge, Crowley, and surrounding areas.

Can I be added without a subscription?

Reach out to hello@thevendoredit.com — we occasionally add vendors manually for editorial reasons.
