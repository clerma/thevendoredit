Who We Are
Curated for Acadiana

The Event Edit was created to give couples in Lafayette and the surrounding Acadiana region a better way to discover exceptional wedding vendors.

We believe your wedding vendors should be as curated as your aesthetic — which is why every listing on The Event Edit is hand-selected to meet our standard of quality, professionalism, and local expertise.

Whether you're searching for a photographer who captures authentic emotion, a florist with a sophisticated eye, or a DJ who knows how to keep a Louisiana crowd dancing, you'll find them here.
