The Calendar

When to marry
in Acadiana.

Spring
March–May. Camellias, magnolia bloom, mid-70s. Best for garden ceremonies and farm venues before humidity arrives.
Popularity · High
Summer
June–August. Hot and humid. Book indoor venues, or lean into it with evening ceremonies and plantation homes with porches.
Popularity · Low
Fall
September–November. The peak season. Pecan harvest, golden light, 70s-and-falling weather. Book 12 months out.
Popularity · Peak
Winter
December–February. Mild by national standards (50s–60s). Romantic for candlelit indoor receptions; great vendor availability.
Popularity · Med
