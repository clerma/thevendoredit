A Real Wedding · October 2024

Emma & James,
at The Magnolia Ballroom.

A romantic garden wedding at The Magnolia Ballroom in Lafayette, Louisiana — blooms in blush and ivory, candlelight, and dancing well past midnight.

Photography · Southern Lens Photography Venue · The Magnolia Ballroom Lafayette, Louisiana

Emma and James celebrated their love surrounded by lush florals and warm candlelight at The Magnolia Ballroom in Lafayette. This romantic garden wedding was a dream come true — blooms in blush and ivory, a live string quartet for the ceremony, and dancing that lasted well past midnight.

The couple wanted their wedding to feel intimate despite their 200-person guest list, and every detail was carefully considered to create that sense of warmth. From the hand-lettered escort cards to the locally baked wedding cake, the day was a love letter to Lafayette and everyone who has been part of their story.
