Bubbli Balloons is a custom balloon decoration company born in the heart of Cajun Country — Lafayette, Louisiana. Founded in 2018 and operated by four southern women with a shared love of parties and creative design, Bubbli brings energy, craftsmanship, and a deeply personal touch to every installation they create. Delivery and travel services are available throughout the Lafayette area and beyond.

Their specialty is organic balloon artistry: lush garlands, oversized arches, and bespoke installations designed around your event's color palette and aesthetic. Named designs like Champagne Dreams Organic Garland, Merlot Organic Garland, and Splash of Wine Organic Garland reflect their eye for detail and their commitment to creating something that feels curated, not generic. Whether it's an intimate dinner party, a grand wedding reception, or a corporate activation, Bubbli designs with intention.

They serve weddings, birthday parties, bridal showers, corporate events, and any celebration worth marking with something beautiful. Browse their Instagram portfolio at @bubbliballoons to see their work in full color.
