Luxury Limo of Lafayette is a premium ground transportation company serving Lafayette and the surrounding South Louisiana region with a fleet built for special occasions. Whether you need a sleek executive vehicle for a small wedding party or a full-capacity party bus for the whole crew, they have the right vehicle and a professional driver to get you there in style.

Their fleet includes a Mercedes Benz Sprinter with limo-style seating, a Cadillac Escalade SUV, multiple party buses accommodating up to 26 passengers, and — most distinctively — a Pearl White Wedding Trolley with a balcony, making it one of the most memorable arrival options available in Acadiana for wedding days. All vehicles are maintained to a high standard and driven by experienced, reliable professionals.

Beyond weddings, Luxury Limo handles airport transfers, casino excursions, homecoming and prom transportation, birthday and anniversary celebrations, and general nights out. Reservations can be made online, and the team is available Monday through Friday 9am–5pm and Saturdays by appointment.
