Carlos and Ashley Lerma are a husband-and-wife photography team based in Opelousas, Louisiana, specializing in weddings, engagements, portraits, and lifestyle photography throughout Lafayette and the surrounding Acadiana region. Originally from Texas — Houston and Austin respectively — they met in Lafayette, married here, and fell in love with the warmth and culture of Cajun country, which shapes everything about the way they work and the images they create.

Their style is modern, creative, and deeply personal. For weddings, they always shoot together as a two-photographer team, giving you comprehensive coverage from getting ready through the last dance. Two perspectives, two lenses, and two people who genuinely care about your day — that's the Carlos and Ashley difference. Engagement sessions, family and senior portraits, and lifestyle photography are also part of their roster, with a single-shooter option for smaller sessions.

They also photograph corporate and commercial events including conferences and trade shows. Packages are custom-quoted based on your specific needs, with a 30% retainer required at booking and the balance due two weeks before the event. Wedding galleries are delivered within 12 weeks.
