Get Lit With Marquee is an event rental and balloon décor company serving Acadiana, Lafayette, and Baton Rouge with one of the largest illuminated marquee inventories in the region. Their collection spans nearly 80 letters (A through Z) and approximately 40 numbers (0 through 9), each standing 4 feet tall and constructed from 3/4" MDF with precision CNC craftsmanship. Whether you need a couple's initials glowing at the reception entrance or full word displays throughout the venue, they can make it happen.

Alongside their marquee inventory, Get Lit With Marquee creates fully custom balloon installations hand-crafted using professional-grade biodegradable brands — Qualatex, Gemar, Tuftex, Betallic, and Anagram. Their organic garlands and arches are designed and installed for residential, corporate, and event venues. Backdrop rentals and custom neon signs round out their offerings, making them a versatile one-stop shop for statement event décor.

Marquee letters start at $65 each and numbers at $50 each. Balloon installations start at $200. Owner Courtney and her team serve Lafayette, Baton Rouge, New Iberia, Franklin, and the broader Acadiana region, with select events reaching New Orleans and Houma.
