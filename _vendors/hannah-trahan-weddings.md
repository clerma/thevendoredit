Hannah Trahan Weddings, operating as Southern Couture Weddings, Events & Honeymoons, is a full-service wedding and event planning firm with over 20 years of experience creating luxury celebrations in Lafayette, Louisiana. Owner Hannah Trahan and her team of five coordinators are specialists in private residence and tent weddings — the kind of events that require vision, precision, and a planner who has seen everything and can handle anything.

Their philosophy is simple but everything: they understand the small details that make the biggest difference, and they work with passion to bring your ideas to life while you actually enjoy your engagement and your wedding day. Services are offered in three tiers — Full-Service Wedding Planning & Design (soup to nuts, concept through execution), Wedding Day Management (your coordinator on-site handling every moving part), and Directed Planning (a flexible hybrid for couples who want guidance without full handoff).

Beyond weddings, the Southern Couture team plans engagement parties, bridal showers, birthday celebrations, corporate events, and Mardi Gras events throughout Acadiana. Hannah is also a certified travel agent through My Paradise Planner, providing honeymoon and destination vacation planning as a seamless complement to their wedding services.
