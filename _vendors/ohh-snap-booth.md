Ohh Snap Booth is a modern photo booth and 360 experience company based in Lafayette, Louisiana, bringing studio-quality photography to weddings and celebrations across South Louisiana. With five distinct booth options and a team that handles every detail from setup to takedown, they make it easy for guests to walk away with a keepsake that lasts long after the reception ends.

Their lineup spans the full range of what a photo experience can be: a classic Portrait Booth with professional lighting, a Social Booth selfie station for digital-first guests, a 360 Photo Booth for video content and social sharing, a Roaming Photo Booth with an on-site photographer who moves through your crowd, and a Virtual Photo Booth for hybrid events. Every package includes an on-site attendant, customizable digital templates matched to your event aesthetic, and instant sharing via text, email, and social media.

Packages start at $500 for a 2-hour digital experience and go up to $900 for a full 3-hour Ultimate package with unlimited prints, animated GIFs, and an online gallery. They serve Lafayette and the surrounding South Louisiana area including Scott, New Iberia, Breaux Bridge, and beyond — and have earned a perfect 5-star rating from their clients.
