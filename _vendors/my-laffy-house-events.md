My Laffy House Events is a boutique event planning and design studio based in Lafayette, Louisiana, founded and operated by Suellen Orellana. Originally from Honduras and now more than 20 years rooted in Lafayette, Suellen holds a Bachelor's degree in Interior Design with a minor in Architecture — and it shows. Her background gives My Laffy House Events a distinctive edge: every event she designs is treated as a space, with attention to proportion, palette, and atmosphere that most planners simply don't bring to the table.

Her event services run the full spectrum from concept to execution: event conceptualization, venue selection, vendor coordination, logistics management, and on-site supervision throughout your wedding day. Florals, centerpieces, catering coordination, backdrops, and tableware are all part of the offering. The same studio also handles residential and commercial interior redesigns under the My LaffyHouse brand, so if you're looking for someone who thinks holistically about how a space feels, Suellen is your person.

Suellen's personal travels across multiple countries have shaped a design sensibility that draws from global influences while remaining grounded in what each individual client actually wants. The result is events that feel both elevated and deeply personal — never cookie-cutter. She serves weddings, corporate gatherings, conferences, and social celebrations throughout the Lafayette area.
